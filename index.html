  • CSCI 2113: Software Engineering (Fall '22)
  • CSCI 1112: Data Structures and Algorithms (Spring '22)

In Fall 2022 and Spring 2023, I am co-taught an original 1-credit elective with a fellow student, titled Internet Sociology. The course was a discussion-based seminar on contemporary critiques of Internet software design and architecture, with readings from scholars in the fields of computer science, philosophy, cognitive psychology, economics, and political science.
